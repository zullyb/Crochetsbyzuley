About me

Hello, I'm Zuley. I work in tec as a Database Administrator with a passion for creativity. Though my work is rooted in tech, I’ve always been drawn to hands-on hobbies like keyboard building, reading books, drawing, and digital art. That creative spark eventually led me to crochet, and it has been my favorite hobby ever since.

I started crocheting on Thanksgiving two years ago, inspired by a TikTok video and a spontaneous Walmart run. As a lefty, learning was tough, and I made plenty of mistakes, but I stuck with it. Crocheting has since become a source of calm and a way to manage anxiety and depression.

Now, I am excited to share my crochet journey with you. I love connecting with others, exchanging ideas, and encouraging creativity. Thanks for stopping by and welcome to my crochet world!
