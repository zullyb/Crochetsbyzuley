About Me

Hello, I’m Zuley. I work in tech as a Database Administrator with a passion for creativity.

Even though my work is rooted in tech, I’ve always had a creative side. That spark led me to crochet, and it quickly became something I truly love.

I started crocheting on Thanksgiving two years ago after being inspired by a TikTok video and a spontaneous Walmart run. Learning as a lefty was not easy, but I stayed with it.

Crochet has become a calming and creative outlet for me, and now I’m excited to share my journey and creations with others.
